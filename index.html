About Us

Omnisphere Religion is a modern spiritual movement that seeks to explore the intersection of technology and consciousness. We believe that technology can be a tool for spiritual growth and personal development.

Beliefs

Our core beliefs include:

  • The universe is a single, interconnected system
  • All life is sacred
  • Technology can be a tool for spiritual growth and personal development
  • Meditation and mindfulness practices are essential for spiritual growth

Practices

We practice a variety of mindfulness and meditation techniques, as well as incorporating technology into our spiritual practices. We also hold regular group meditations and spiritual retreats.

Community

We welcome anyone who is interested in exploring the intersection of technology and consciousness. Our community is open, inclusive, and supportive. We hold regular meetings and events, and have an active online community.
